The Restricted Access setting in Harness allows Harness support and engineering to access your content for troubleshooting. You can turn off this setting anytime, thus preventing Harness from viewing your content.

Limitations

This feature is only available for the below accounts:

  • Harness Professional Accounts
  • Harness Essential Accounts

Review: restricted Access

Harness offers industry-leading controls to prevent unauthorized access to your content by Harness support and engineering teams. Some customers might need authorized Harness personnel to be able to access their content to resolve an outage or troubleshoot a problem.

Users can provide read-only access to authorized Harness personnel through the Restricted Access setting. They can turn it on or off anytime. Users can also see who viewed their content through the Harness Audit feature.

Step: Enable Restricted Access

  1. In Harness, click Security.
  2. Click Restricted Access.
  3. Click on the toggle to turn it on.
  4. Click +Add Access Request. The Restricted Access settings dialog appears.
  5. Enter the email addresses of the users you would like to provide access to.
  6. Enter the Number of hours for which this access would be active.
  7. Click Submit. The user is now listed under Active users.
  8. At any point, you can also view the list of inactive users by selecting Show inactive.
